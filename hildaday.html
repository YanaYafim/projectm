Hilda
2018-2023 | 6+ | 24min | UK Canada USA

Dir. Andy Coyle

Starring actors: Bella Ramsay , Amira Falzon-Ojo , Oliver Nelson , Daisy Haggard and others

The animated series, tells the story of a little girl, Hilda, who lives with her mother in a house on a plain surrounded by mountains and forests. The life of a fearless girl and her faithful companion Twig is filled with mysterious events. But soon, a forced move from the house next to the elf settlement to Trollberg helps Hilda not only find new friends, but also discover the world of many creatures previously unknown to her. True, not all of them are as harmless as it seems at first glance.
